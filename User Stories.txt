MONEY CONTROL - USER STORIES 

Essential:
As a user, I would like to be able to enter my expenses and group them into categories.
As a user, I would like to add date and timestamp on every expense I enter.
As a user, I would like to select expenses based on filters like category, date and time, monthly and yearly.  
As a user, I would like to have a feature of adding notes, bullet points and other details for every expense I add.
As a user, I would like to have an option to choose the method of payment.
As a user, I would like to have a repeat expense option for me to repeat an expense from the list of expenditures.

Necessary:
As a user, I would like to have icons assigned to each category.
As a user, I would like to have an option of updating and/or deleting my transaction entries when required.
As a user, I would like to see the list of repeated transactions to choose from when I am entering a new transaction.
As a user, I would like to have a feature for overall budget planning and also be able to set category level thresholds.
As a user, I would like to have a dedicated tab to enter incomes from all different sources.


Nice to have:
As a user, I would like to see my expenses in the form of graphs or charts, to enable me to have a percentage overview. 
As a user, I would like to have a help section that gives me overview of how to use the application.
As a user, I would like to have insights about my spending habits, including categories in which I spend most on a weekly/monthly/early basis.
As a user, I would like the app to import/export my weekly/monthly/yearly details of entries.
As a user, I would like to select the currency type when I enter the transaction and convert to default currency that I have set.
